Welcome With this website, you can turn Happy Wheels art shapes into
smooth rectangle segments.
Instructions Open Happy Wheels and go to the level editor. Select the Art Tool, draw a path and press Enter. Copy the Level XML of your level and paste it in the textarea. Adjust the sliders and press the "Copy Level XML" button. Paste the copied Level XML in the level editor and enjoy!
